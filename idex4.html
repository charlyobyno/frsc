Special Marshals

ESTABLISHMENT

The Special Marshal are the volnteer arm of the Federal Road Safety Corps. This volunteer arm was created by the same FRSC statute Sec. 10(1)..."the Corp" which shall consist of the such nmber of uniform and non-niform members as may be determined from time to time, by the commission. FRSC Establishment Act (2007). They are men and women of means, with proven integrity in the society and able to influence their immediate environment (work place/community) in favor of the course of road safety. The Act empowers the special Marshals to carry ot patrol and other activities that ensre good road usage on our highways. Just like Regularconterparts, they can arrest and book traffic offenders as well as prosecte them when neccessary.
