What We Do
In February 1988, the federal Government created the federal Road Safety Commission through Decree 35 of 1992 referred to in the statute books as the FRSC Act Cap 141 Laws of the Federation of Nigeria (LFN). Passed by the National Assemble as Federal Road Safety Commission (establishment) Act 2007.

The Corps Vision

To eradicate road traffic crashes and create safe monitoring environment in Nigeria.

Mission Statement

The Mission of the corpe is to Regulate, Enforce and Coordinate all Road Traffic and Safety Management activities through: ● Sustained public enlightenment ● Robust data Management ● Improved vehicle Administration ● Prompt rescue services ● Effective patrol operation.

In particular, the Commisison is charged with the responsibilities for:

  • Preventing or minimizing accidents on the highway;
  • Clearing obstrctions on any part of the highways;
  • Educating drivers, motorists and other members of the public generally on the proper se of the highway
  • Designing and producing the driver's license to be used by various categories of vehicle operators;
  • Determine, from the time to time, the reqirements to be satisfied by an applicant for a driver's licence;
